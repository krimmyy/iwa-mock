Welcome to black & white Photography

Hi,
I am Kassy and I am the photographer behind Black & White Photography.
I specialise in portraiture and fashion as I love to capture the character of others.

I first picked up a camera in around 2006 and decided to pursue this further, so attended college in 2007-09.
since that time I have worked with many individuals and gained a lot of experiences and have decided to pursue my love for photography, full time as a business.

So if you’d like an award winning, experienced Northern Ireland Photographer for any kind of project please get in touch. Arrange an appointment, enjoy a nice coffee and view some of our work.

We’d love to have a chat! The best way to contact us is by using our form. Pick the form that suits what you’re looking for.
